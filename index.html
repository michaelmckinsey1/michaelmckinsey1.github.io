My Resume

My ideal job is to work for a company where Software Development is the main product. I want the development that I do to be high-value to not only the customers, but to the company as well. I am not particularly partial to a specific area of computer science. I really enjoyed the project I worked on last summer because it was challenging and fruitful, not necessarily because of the area it was in. I believe there are opportunities like this in every area, and so I don't want to limit myself to a single space.

I am confident programming in C++ and Python, but I don't think there is any language that I can't/won't learn, even on the fly. I see languages as a means to an end: it's a matter of learning what you need to about a tool to accomplish your goal. I feel like I have the right amount of experience and coursework at this point to try and tackle any challenge, even if I need to work at it a little harder.
